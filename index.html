Writer's Marketplace is...

Writer's Marketplace is more than just a tool... it is a community where writers of all kinds, song writers, poets, screenwriters, authors, and more can post their work for review and support. They can monetize their works and be provided with improvements thanks to AI technology.


















Share and Monetize Your Works

Using Ethereum, writers are able to post and value their works. When purchased the writer gets 85% of the profits and the buyer recieves all the rights to the work. The buyer and seller may negotiate on price and unethcial transactions will be punished.

















Get Feedback

Utilizing Verbwire, Writer's Marketplace is able analyze your writing and provide constructive feedback to increase your credibility and skill as a writer of any type or genre

















Share and Store

Blockchain is applied to create a safe and fair way to store and share stories and writings without relying on a single authority. This helps to prevent tampering and censorship of the content, while also providing benefits for writers and readers.
